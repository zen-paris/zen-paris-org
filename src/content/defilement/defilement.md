<p>Buddhism [of the Tendai school] presents women as incapable of attaining Buddhahood because of this defilement by blood [from menstruation and childbirth] and as the bearers of sins that cause them to fall into the underworld. If they are to attain Buddhahood, they must take on male bodies.</p>
<p>Haruko Wakita – L’histoire des femmes au Japon</p>
